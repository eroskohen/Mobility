Fatfuk Mobility

Let’s keep it real. If you sit all day, skip warmups, and treat stretching like it’s optional, your body’s gonna fold in on itself like Quasimodo at a desk job.

This routine is built for anyone who’s tight, stiff, sore, or just plain tired of moving like a creaky folding chair. No fluff, no yoga-nonsense — just the movements you actually need to undo the damage.

Do these consistently and you’ll feel looser, stronger in weird positions, and less like a fatfuck statue locked in 90° angles. Train smart. Move well. Stay human.

Shoulders & Upper Body

Shoulder Dislocate
Band rotation front to back with locked arms. Opens chest and shoulders.
Banded Shoulder Hold
Straight-arm isometric chest press with a resistance band.
Prayer Lat Stretch
Child’s pose variation with arms reaching to sides. Targets lats and T-spine.
Shoulder External Rotation Block
Face-down external shoulder rotation with hand on block.
Prone Y Raise
Lying Y-shaped arm raises. Strengthens lower traps and scapular control.
Prone Arm Sweep
Lift, rotate, and sweep arm behind the back. Promotes scapular mobility.

Back & Spine

Cat Cow
Spinal flexion/extension on all fours. Improves back articulation.
Thread The Needle
Reach-under twist stretch. Opens thoracic spine and lats.
Cobra Pose
Prone press-up. Opens spine and hip flexors.
Open Book
Side-lying twist with knee across body. Mobilizes T-spine.
Dead Hang
Passive hang from a bar. Decompresses spine and shoulders.

Hips & Legs

90 90
Hip internal/external rotation drill. Improves joint control.
Couch Stretch
Quad and hip flexor stretch with back foot elevated.
Elephant Walks
Alternating hamstring stretch. Improves posterior chain mobility.
Elevated Pidgeon
Glute and piriformis stretch using a raised surface.
Butterfly Pulse
Groin opener using bouncing knees. Targets inner hips.
Frog Pose
Deep groin stretch with wide knees on the floor.
Hip Cars
Controlled hip rotations. Builds joint strength and control.
Hip Flexor Block Lıft
Seated hip lift over block. Strengthens hip flexors.
Monster Walk
Banded side steps. Activates glute medius.
Greatest Stretch
Deep lunge with T-spine twist and elbow drop.
Seated Pancake
Wide-leg seated fold. Stretches hamstrings and adductors.
Horse Pose
Isometric wide squat. Builds adductor strength.
Cossack Squat
Side-to-side deep squats. Targets hips, hamstrings, and ankles.

Feet & Calves

Ankle Dorsilexion
Knee-to-wall stretch. Improves ankle mobility.
Plantar Fascia Rolling
Roll bottom of foot with ball. Loosens foot fascia.
Side To Side Shift Squat
Deep squat weight shifts. Opens hips and calves.
Seiza Pose
Sit on heels. Stretches quads and ankles.
